Judgment aid

Final checks before departure

  • Refresh Met Office shipping and inshore waters immediately before leaving.
  • Check whether any strong wind warning remains in force.
  • Compare actual harbour/nearby wind with forecast gusts.
  • Use the tidal stream plate closest to your time relative to high water Devonport.
  • For a family sail, treat gusts above F4/F5 comfort as a reason to shorten or stay sheltered.
